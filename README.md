## ğŸ“ˆ Key KPIs & Insights

Based on the attached visuals, the following insights were derived:

1. **Orders vs Sales Trend**
   * Both **order volume and total sales show a declining trend** over the selected period, indicating reduced customer demand or seasonal slowdown.
2. **Gender-wise Sales Contribution**
   * **Women contribute ~64% of total sales**, making them the dominant customer segment.
3. **Order Status Analysis**
   * **Returned and cancelled orders form a negligible portion**, indicating strong order fulfillment and customer satisfaction.
4. **Top 5 States by Sales**
   * Sales are concentrated in the **top 5 performing states**, highlighting key regional markets driving revenue.
5. **Age & Gender Analysis**
   * The **Adult age group generates the highest sales**, across both genders, showing strong purchasing power in this segment.
6. **Order Channel Performance**
   * Majority of orders are generated through **Flipkart and Amazon**, followed by platforms like **Ajio**, indicating marketplace-driven sales dominance.
